Candle in the Dark Church

About Us

Candle in the Dark Church is a vibrant Christian ministry under the Dag Heward-Mills Ministry (DHMM), a global movement dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ through evangelism, church planting, and discipleship. As part of this worldwide vision, Candle in the Dark Church serves as a beacon of hope, illuminating lives with the Word of God. Its headquarters is in Milan, Cologno Nord, Italy, and it has several branches across Italy and six other European countries: France, Spain, Belgium, Germany, Hungary, and Budapest. Through dynamic preaching, practical teachings, and spirit-filled worship, the church nurtures believers to grow in faith and fulfill their divine purpose. Candle in the Dark Church operates under the leadership of Bishop Immanuel Obeng, a devoted servant of God and convenor of the ministry. He carries the same vision of DHMM to establish strong, Bible-based churches that transform communities through the power of the Holy Spirit. As part of DHMM, Candle in the Dark Church shares in the mission of raising leaders, sending missionaries, and impacting the world with the message of salvation. Inspired by the teachings of Evangelist Dag Heward-Mills, the church remains committed to evangelism, healing, and equipping believers to shine as lights in a dark world.
