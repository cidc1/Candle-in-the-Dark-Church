About Us

Candle in the Dark Church is a Christian ministry under the Dag Heward-Mills Ministry (DHMM), dedicated to spreading the Gospel through evangelism, church planting, and discipleship. Led by Bishop Immanuel Obeng, the church has its headquarters in Milan, Cologno Nord, Italy, with branches across France, Spain, Belgium, Germany, Hungary, and Budapest. Through spirit-filled worship and practical teachings, it nurtures believers to grow in faith and fulfill their divine purpose. Committed to raising leaders, sending missionaries, and transforming communities through the power of the Holy Spirit, the church follows the vision of DHMM and the teachings of Evangelist Dag Heward-Mills.
